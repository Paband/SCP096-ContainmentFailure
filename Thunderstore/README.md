# SCP096: Containment Failure
![Logo](https://i.imgur.com/LtRg5e7.png)
Adds the ***Shy Guy*** (SCP-096) into Lethal Company.
Fully updated and maintained for current versions of Lethal Company!
Originally created by [Jasper.Creations](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/jaspercreations/), maintained by [TheUnknownCod3r](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/theunknowncod3r/), now forked and updated by [junkeagle943](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/junkeagle943/)!

# Features
- Fully custom enemy behavior and animation.

![close call LOL](https://i.imgur.com/szyWFSu.gif)
- Configurable sounds and appearance
	+ Switch between sounds from SCP: Containment Breach and SCP: Secret Laboratory
- Configurable values and speeds.
- Fully configurable spawn rates, allowing the Shy Guy to spawn outside on higher-level moons.
- Will always track its targets down, and only its targets, **no matter what**.
- Custom Break in Animation for the Ship
- Spawnable Painting scrap
- Can Escape via both the Fire Exit, or Main Entrance.
- **NEW: Poltergeist Support** — Dead players who pester the Shy Guy will trigger his rage state, targeting the nearest living player!

![no matter what](https://i.imgur.com/BgdQZZ3.gif)

# Backstory
The Shy Guy is based on [SCP-096](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-096) by Dr Dan, from the SCP wiki.

**Description:** SCP-096 is a humanoid creature measuring approximately 2.38 meters in height. Subject shows very little muscle mass, with preliminary analysis of body mass suggesting mild malnutrition. Arms are grossly out of proportion with the rest of the subject's body, with an approximate length of 1.5 meters each. Skin is mostly devoid of pigmentation, with no sign of any body hair.

SCP-096 is normally extremely docile. However, when someone views SCP-096's face, whether it be directly, via video recording, or even a photograph, it will enter a stage of considerable emotional distress. Approximately one to two minutes after the first viewing, SCP-096 will begin running to the person who viewed its face. No known material or method can impede SCP-096's progress.
